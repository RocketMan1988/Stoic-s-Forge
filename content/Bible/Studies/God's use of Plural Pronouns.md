---
Date: 04/23/222022 20:04

### Summary
There are generally 5 theories on use of plural pronouns in [[Gen-01#v26]]
1) The [[Trinity]]
2) God and his Angels
3) God and Creation
4) [[God's majesty]] as expressed by a creation
5) A [[Polytheistic]] god
